Services and Facilities

  • MediChain is a modular and scalable website-based solution for managing queuing in Outpatient Departments (OPDs), tracking bed availability, and managing patient admissions in hospitals.
  • The use of location-based services to suggest nearby hospitals adds a layer of convenience and personalization
  • The system reduces the stress and time involved in finding a hospital bed, especially in emergencies.
  • We ensure that patients have access to the most current information on bed availability.

Imapct and Benefits

  • Patients can check their queue status and expected wait times online, allowing them to plan their arrival more accurately, reducing the time spent in waiting areas.
  • The ability to register online reduces the hassle of in-person registration, especially for non-critical cases.
  • In emergencies, patients can be quickly routed to the nearest available facility, potentially saving lives.
  • The system can also help hospitals balance their patient load by directing patients to less crowded facilities, preventing overburdening.
  • Data collected can inform policy decisions and help in planning future healthcare infrastructure needs.
